STAY CONNECTED WITH OHIO STATE
 
 
 

Dear Bri,

I want to offer you my hearty congratulations on earning a degree from Ohio State! As a fellow alumnus, I know the great pride you feel when earning a degree and am honored to welcome you into the worldwide network of alumni Buckeyes that’s more than half a million strong. Now you're a member, too.

There are a variety of ways to stay involved with your alma mater. Discover special programming for young alumni, keep connected with the Ohio State app, volunteer with fellow alumni or make a gift to support Ohio State students and become a sustaining member. Be sure to visit the alumni portal (log in using your name.# Ohio State username) to update your information, check your giving history and more. There are so many ways to stay connected!

We look forward to having you as part of the alumni family.

Sincerely, and Go Bucks!
Andy Gurd
Interim President / CEO
Chief Operating Officer
The Ohio State University Alumni Association
