About me

Marketing professional by day, wedding stationer and house-builder by night

I am a marketing professional with expertise in email marketing, digital strategy, content creation and management, email and web analytics, writing, social media management and design. I excel in a collaborative, goal-oriented environment and that is innovative and data-driven.

In my current role as Email Marketing Manager at The Ohio State University, I orchestrate email programs for the university's various audiences, and co-lead the university's active email marketing community. I’ve worked in digital marketing at Ohio State for 8 years and have done everything from story writing and social media management, to website project management and campaign execution. As a self-proclaimed email geek, I have a special place in my heart for the challenges and possibilities email marketing presents. I’ve dedicated the last three years to email marketing, creating and deploying, measuring and iterating.

I’m happiest spending time analyzing performance, identifying opportunities, and creating test-and-learn strategies for continuous improvement. I also have a passion for educating others and supporting the growth of talented marketers.
