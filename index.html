About me

I am a marketing professional with expertise in email marketing, digital strategy, content creation and management, email and web analytics, writing, social media management and design. I excel in a collaborative, goal-oriented environment that is innovative and data-driven.

In my current role as Associate Director of Email Marketing at The Ohio State University, I develop email programs for the university's various audiences and co-lead the university's active email marketing community. As a self-proclaimed email geek, I have a special place in my heart for the challenges and possibilities email marketing presents. I’ve worked in digital marketing at Ohio State for eight years and have done everything from story writing and social media management, to website project management and campaign execution.

I’m happiest spending time analyzing performance, identifying opportunities, and creating test-and-learn strategies for continuous improvement. I also have a passion for educating others and supporting the growth of talented marketers.

Outside of work, I’m working toward my MBA, checking off home projects with my husband in our newly built home, binging on Netflix or exploring new places.

Expertise

    BRANDED EMAIL TOOLKIT

    Branded email templates and assets

    Branded email templates walkthrough

    I led an email template project to improve brand consistency and workflow efficiency. The goal was to provide a more consistent audience experience so recipients could immediately recognize an Ohio State email in their inbox. We accomplished this by creating easy-to-use, modular-based templates to accommodate various marketing needs, and by providing email brand guidelines and a how-to-guide for easy reference along with in-person workshops.

    DAY OF GIVING EMAIL

    Dynamic email to donors

    To promote the Day of Giving to donors in a personalized way, we created a dynamic email that displayed content and associated funds from recipients’ top two giving areas. This approach led to a 140% increase in conversion rate over the previous year’s donor email.

    Ohio State Insights

    Thought leadership and research newsletter

    This subscription-based, weekly newsletter features research content from thought leaders across the university. Content falls into one of eight categories, and through user behavior we're able to track interest and serve up dynamic content based on those preferences.

    Connect Newsletter

    Monthly donor nurturing newsletter

    This monthly newsletter features philanthropic and donor impact stories to nurture current donors and to inspire future donors. In this particular issue, we teased a video of the newly rennovated Mirror Lake with a gif and play button overlay. We've seen repeated lift in engagement when using this technique.

    VISIT SITE REDESIGN

    An audience-centered approach

    In an effort to improve the user experience, the project team set out to update visit.osu.edu. I led the information architecture piece, utilizing historical web analytics to identify most visited pages, user paths and behavior. From that the team formulated a user-centered navigation which changes elements of content on the home page to meet the needs of different types of visitors.
